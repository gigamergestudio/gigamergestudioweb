What is GigaMerge Studio?

Core Idea

We bridge talented creators (editors, coders, writers) with clients who need work done. We manage projects, ensure quality, and handle payments — acting as a mini production company + freelance agency.

How It Works

Recruit → Find clients → Receive project → Assign to team → Deliver → Payment → Distribute earnings. Simple, scalable, and powered by our ecosystem.
